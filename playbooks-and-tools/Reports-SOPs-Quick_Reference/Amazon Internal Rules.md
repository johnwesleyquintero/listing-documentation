## 4. SEO & Keyword Strategy

*   **Leverage Avi's Structure:** Utilize the simplified and structured bullet points provided by Avi for inherent search visibility.
*   **Image Meta Tags:** Add relevant keywords as meta tags (alt text) to all images (A+ Content, main images, etc.) for enhanced indexing and SEO. Adhere strictly to the **100-character limit** for alt text.
*   **Backend Keywords:** Populate backend search terms with generic, high-impact keywords, avoiding commas and repetition, adhering to Amazon's character limits (typically 250 characters).
